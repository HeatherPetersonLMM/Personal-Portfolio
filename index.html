GLYDE CONDOMS

Re-Design for Glyde's website

They wanted to re-design their website into something that was modern, user-friendly and clean but also a website that felt organic.

Project: Freelance
Client: Glyde

MCHAPPY DAY

Facebook App For Mcdonalds Russia

20 November 2012 was the eleventh annual charity event "McHappy Day", which has raised more than 17 million rubles for the construction of Russia's first "Ronald McDonald House" - a real "home away from home" for families whose children receive long-term treatment at the Children's Republican Hospital in Kazan.
The Facebook App was built to spread awareness of the charity event and that people could donate money by buying a ring that was made by one of the children and showing their support by have the ring as a badge on Facebook.

This app was a part of a bigger campaign that won the Cannes Lions Bronze in Retail/E-Commerce.

Client: Mcdonalds Russia
Agency: Leo Burnett Moscow
AD: David A Ljungberg
Project: Freelance
Role: Visual Designer

KEEP CALM & GRIND COFFEE

Coffee Jar - Typography

Coffee is one of my biggest passions in life and I store my coffee beans in a boring old jar. So on day I decided to make that jar look more appealing and one of a kind.

I based my typography sketch on the famous poster from WWII - Keep Calm and Carry On, but of course with my own twist on it. So I ended up with the text Keep Calm & Grind. Beside painting the Jar I also made a wallpaper with the same text on it.

Project: Personal

GUSTAV VASA TEE

T-shirt Design - Swedish National Day

Madeinthenow.com contacted me and gave me an offer I couldn't refuse. I got the pleasure to make a Tee for the celebration of the Swedish National Day.

June 6 marks the annual celebration of The National Day of Sweden. One of the reasons we celebrate National Day on this day is in memory and honour of King Gustav Vasa. Who was elected King of Sweden on June 6 1523. He is considered the founder of modern Sweden and also the one who ended the war with the Danish.

Project: Personal
For: www.madeinthenow.com

ABSOLUT BROOKLYN

Tactile Illustration

This was my final project at Hyper Island.
I wanted to try something new and something that would challenge me and also analogue and not just digital. Ever since I had my work shadowing at Shotopop LTD in London I wanted to try to make a tactile illustration, They really inspired and woke my interested for this kind of illustration. I built my idea around the awesome bottle Absolut Brooklyn which were designed by Spike Lee.

Hello, My name is Andreas Knutsson. I'm a 27 year old Interactive & Graphic Designer from Sweden. I'm a coffee addicted pixel pusher who loves design in all it's shapes and forms and to work with concepts.

As a former student of the renowned school Hyper Island, I studied Digital Media and interned as a visual designer at HUGE, Brooklyn New York. At HUGE I worked on Target and on several critical pitches.

Right now I'm working as a freelance designer based out of Stockholm and mostly doing projects together with my friends at Seven Seas Collective in the U.S.

Beside my passion for design and to create new things, I’m also a big sport freak and a big foodie who loves the good things in life. As in design, I love to create and to try new things in the kitchen.
